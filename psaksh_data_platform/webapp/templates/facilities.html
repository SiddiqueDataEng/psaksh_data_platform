DHQ, RHC and BHU assessments across 36 districts — readiness scores, staffing gaps, and commodity stock-outs

Facility Readiness Scoring: Each facility is scored 0–100 based on staffing (present vs. required), infrastructure (electricity, water, sanitation), and essential medicines (ORS, Zinc, Amoxicillin, Iron-Folate, Vitamin A, Oxytocin). Facilities scoring below 60% are classified as "not ready" and prioritised for supply-chain and HR interventions. BHUs in Balochistan consistently score lowest.
