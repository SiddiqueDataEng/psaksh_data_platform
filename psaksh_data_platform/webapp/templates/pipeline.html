⚙ Data Pipeline — Medallion Architecture

Bronze → Silver → Gold  |  Full Load / Incremental / CDC / SCD2 / Windowing / Delta Log

🏗 The 3-Layer Data Journey

Raw survey data enters the Bronze layer as-is — exact copy, metadata added, no transformation. The Silver layer applies 10 DQ cleaning rules: bilingual normalisation, deduplication, schema evolution, CDC tagging. The Gold layer produces SCD2 dimensions, fact tables, and windowed KPI aggregations consumed by Flask, Streamlit, Power BI, and Tableau. Every run is logged in the Delta Log with watermarks, checksums, and lineage metadata.
