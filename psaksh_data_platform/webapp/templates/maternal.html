🤱 Maternal Health

Antenatal care coverage, skilled birth attendance, and maternal anemia across 36 districts

Why Maternal Health Matters: Women who receive 4+ antenatal care visits are significantly more likely to deliver with a skilled attendant, reducing maternal and neonatal mortality. Maternal anemia (Hb < 11 g/dL) increases the risk of low birth weight, preterm delivery, and child stunting — creating an intergenerational cycle of malnutrition. Balochistan and rural Sindh show the largest gaps in both ANC coverage and skilled delivery.
