📍 Field Operations & Data Quality

Back-check methodology · Enumerator performance · GPS validation · Measurement discrepancy analysis

Back-Check Methodology: A random 10% sample of completed household interviews is re-visited by a senior supervisor within 72 hours of the original enumeration. Measurements are compared against original records using defined thresholds: ±2 cm for height and ±0.5 kg for weight. Discrepancies beyond these thresholds trigger an enumerator review and potential data flagging. GPS coordinates are validated against district boundaries, and duplicate household IDs are automatically detected during nightly ETL runs.
