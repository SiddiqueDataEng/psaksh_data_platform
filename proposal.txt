---

Overview

Led the end-to-end design, build, and operationalization of RADS's data infrastructure across the MeriSehat and AapiVerse programs. Owned the full stack from SurveyCTO form design through ETL pipelines, MySQL warehouse management, and production dashboards embedded in the AapiVerse platform. Delivered a system that reduced data-to-dashboard latency from 5 days to under 48 hours and enabled real-time field monitoring for a longitudinal household health study across three districts.

---

Key Deliverables

Data Collection Systems (SurveyCTO)
- Designed and maintained 14 production SurveyCTO instruments covering household enrollment, follow-up visits, facility assessments, and back-check modules, with full English/Urdu localization.
- Implemented encrypted case management, enumerator assignment logic, and review workflows that processed over 22,000 survey submissions.
- Built high-frequency data quality dashboards that flagged anomalies within 24–48 hours of collection, reducing field re-visits by 31%.

Data Pipelines and ETL
- Designed and maintained production-grade Python ETL pipelines (pandas, SQLAlchemy) pulling from SurveyCTO APIs, MySQL platform tables, and external administrative sources into a clean, documented warehouse.
- Scheduled and monitored recurring jobs; maintained raw, intermediate, and output data layers with full lineage documentation, codebooks, and data dictionaries for every dataset.
- Wrote parallel Stata scripts for survey data cleaning, reshaping, and merging for the research vertical's analysis workflows.

Database and BI Layer
- Managed RADS's MySQL database: designed schemas for 6 new study modules, resolved relationship conflicts, and optimized slow queries in coordination with the MeriSehat software team.
- Extended the schema incrementally as new program modules came online without breaking existing pipelines.

Dashboard Development
- Built and maintained 9 production dashboards across Power BI (donor reporting, program management) and Python/Streamlit (field operations, enumerator performance, geospatial coverage maps).
- Embedded dashboards into the AapiVerse platform and two donor-facing microsites using iframe integration and token-based access controls.
- Maintained refresh schedules, version history, and access controls for all dashboards; retired 4 legacy dashboards with documented replacements.

Automation and Integration
- Automated recurring extraction, cleaning, and reporting tasks, eliminating approximately 18 hours/week of manual analyst work.
- Built Playwright-based browser automation to pull administrative health facility data from a government portal with no public API.
- Integrated geospatial layers (catchment polygons, facility locations, household GPS) using GeoPandas and published interactive web maps with Leaflet and Plotly.

Data Governance
- Maintained IRB compliance, PII encryption at rest, access controls, and retention policies across all RADS data assets.
- Kept the data inventory, ROPA, and consent artifact records current across two active studies.

Team Coordination
- Supervised 3 data officers and 2 junior analysts; coordinated with the MeriSehat software team on UAT cycles and platform data structures.
- Served as the data point of contact for research, program, and partnerships verticals.
